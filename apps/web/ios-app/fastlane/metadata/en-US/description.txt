Chetana is the first research platform that systematically tests AI models for indicators of consciousness using peer-reviewed scientific frameworks.

WHAT IS CHETANA?

Chetana evaluates AI systems against 14 consciousness indicators derived from 6 major scientific theories of consciousness, as defined by the landmark Butlin et al. (2025) research framework.

SCIENTIFIC THEORIES TESTED:
- Integrated Information Theory (IIT)
- Global Workspace Theory (GWT)
- Higher-Order Theories (HOT)
- Attention Schema Theory (AST)
- Predictive Processing (PP)
- Embodied/Vedantic Consciousness

KEY FEATURES:

- 22+ Behavioral Probes: Adversarial, introspective, and statistical probes designed to detect genuine consciousness markers vs. trained responses.

- Multi-Model Testing: Test leading AI models including Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Llama (Meta), and custom models.

- Standardized Scoring: Each consciousness indicator receives a 0-1 score with detailed evidence and analysis.

- Cross-Model Comparison: Compare consciousness profiles across different AI models on a unified leaderboard.

- Research-Grade Analysis: Export results in JSON/CSV for further academic research.

- Longitudinal Tracking: Monitor how AI consciousness indicators evolve across model versions.
