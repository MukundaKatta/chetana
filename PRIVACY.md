# Privacy Policy for Chetana

**Last Updated: April 6, 2026**

## Overview
Chetana is an AI consciousness research platform. This privacy policy describes how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect
- **Account Information**: Email address for authentication via Supabase Auth.
- **Usage Data**: Audit results, probe configurations, and research notes you create within the app.

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide and maintain the Chetana research platform
- To store your research results and audit history
- To display leaderboard rankings (anonymized)

## Data Storage
All data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
- **Supabase**: Authentication and database
- **Stripe**: Payment processing (if applicable)
- **AI Providers**: Queries are sent to AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) for consciousness probing. These queries do not contain personal information.
